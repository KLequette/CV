RESEARCH INTERESTS

I am currently completing my PhD studies in microbiology, with a focus on the development of biofilms in irrigation systems, with a link to wastewater reuse. For my next research work, I wish to continue to immerse myself in the understanding of the mechanisms of microbial ecosystems involved in waste treatment and recovery processes.

EDUCATION

PhD studies in Microbiology (2nd year) 2017 - Present

Environmental biotechnology laboratory (LBE-INRAE) - University of Montpellier SupAgro

Supervisor : Nathalie Wéry & Nassim Ait-Mouheb, within DIIEM team & Optimiste teams

Title of the thesis : Biofilm in microirrigation systems fed with treated wastewater: development kinetics and microbial dynamics

Synopsis : My PhD project aims to better understand the establishment and development of biofilms in irrigation systems fed with recycled wastewater. I am mainly conducting metagenomic studies to characterize the microbial communities associated with biofilm development as well as optical studies. I am also interested in whether these biofilms are potential reservoirs for pathogens.
